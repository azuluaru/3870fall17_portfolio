About Me

I was born on a dark and stormy night... Just kidding!

I am currently a college student at CSU East Bay, pursuing my B.F.A. in Multimedia. Primarily my work focuses around illustration and light photo editing - while I prefer traditional, I'm very open to new media and I'm enjoying learning about the digital side of art!

As an artist, I tend to gravitate toward the detail-oriented and the fantastical. I enjoy science fiction and fantasy, and I often incorporate those elements into my artwork. Visit the Gallery page to see some examples of my work.

If you've got a project you're interested in having me work on, I'd love to hear from you! You can see my contact information below, or fill out the form to give me your email address. I hope to hear from you soon!
